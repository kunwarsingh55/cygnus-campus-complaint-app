FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS!

What is the process of filing a complaint?
There will be upload icon floating on the bottom right of the screen . Click on that icon and you will reach to the complaint form in which you can file the complaint. I hope this will clear your doubt :)
How much time does it take to resolve the issue
It depends upon the department, how much time does they want to resolve it . Along with the priority of that issue
How would i check my complaint status
You can check your complaint status on the complaint table section. There you will see all the filed complaints along with the status
How to login on the site
You will see the signin button on the right corner of the navbar. Click on that button to login
